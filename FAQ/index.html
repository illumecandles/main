Aim to burn your candle up to 4 hours. Just make sure that when you first light your candle that you have trimmed the wick to at least 1cm. On the initial burn, make sure that the wax melts all the way to the edge. Wax has a ‘memory’ and if you only allow a small pool of wax to melt, it will only burn to that diameter in the future. Take some extra time on the first burn and it should burn evenly to the bottom. Why? because we want you to enjoy every second!

All Multi wick candles need a longer burn time because of the wide diameter. Multi wick candles should be burnt approximately 4-6 hours depending on the candle diameter. Letting the candle cool fully allows the wax structure to reform and ensure an even burn. Shorter burn times will only create a narrow wax pool and wasted wax. Burning the candle until the pool of wax melts out to the edge will enable you to utilise all that our gorgeous fragranced multi wick candle has to offer.

We aim to honor all change or cancellation requests but is not guaranteed. If you contact us before the products are shipped, there is a higher probability for successfully changing or cancelling the order per request.

Check that the email address you have used to place your order is correct- this is the most common cause of not receiving your order confirmation. It may also be that your e-mail provider has a spam filter that blocks certain emails so be sure to check that folder as well. Orders placed aim to be shipped within the next business day (during high volume sales periods dispatch can be longer). In the event that a product you ordered is out of stock, you will be informed by support by email.

We ship almost anywhere in the world. For some countries, an additional customs fee may apply. Please contact us if you need additional clarifications.
