The Story

The Illume Candle is an independent candle brand based in County Kerry, Ireland

Through numerous searches, of cleaner alternatives of candles, Megan the Illume candle founder, decided to create her own scented candles. Megan went ahead and started researching into the different types of waxes and fragrances available, after testing and experimenting with different suppliers she decided to go ahead with natural soy wax. Upon further research she discovered cotton core wicks with the combination of the soy wax allowed longer burning non toxic candles. Which are safe to use in workplaces and at home.

Illume Candles Ireland has steadily grown from a home grown Irish business into a recognisable brand that now produces hundreds of candles each week. Although new equipment has been introduced to cope with the large influx of sales, everything is still handmade in the Kerry workshop, each candle is lovingly hand poured and all of the labels designed in-house are printed and then each one carefully attached to the jars by hand. Illume Candles Ireland stocks over twenty stores in Ireland and is available at a farmers’ market near you. Please follow our Instagram posts where we show and highlight our latest work.
